Hi, I'm Jessie!

I am a Front-End Web Developer & Visual Designer with a keen eye for detail.

A passion for creating human centered design has led me on a wild journey from the Fast Fashion Industry to Web Design & Development. I find the increasing demand for accessibility practices in the digital space to be exciting and inspiring. Appreciating problems/limitations as creative opportunities is vital to my design process. I believe that an understanding of functional limitations can help elevate the aesthetics and usability of websites to invite a wider audience.
